FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the minimum age for driving lessons?

The minimum age for driving lessons is 18 years old.

Do you offer automatic car lessons?

Yes, we offer both manual and automatic car lessons.

How long is each driving lesson?

Each driving lesson lasts for 1 hour, but we also offer extended lessons for those who need more time.

Can I learn to drive if I have never driven before?

Yes! Our instructors are trained to work with beginners and guide you through every step of the process.

Do I need to bring my own car to lessons?

No, we provide vehicles for all driving lessons. Our cars are equipped with dual controls for safety.
